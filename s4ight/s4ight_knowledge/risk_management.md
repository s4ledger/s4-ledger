# Risk Management for PMS Programs

## Framework
Risk = Likelihood × Consequence, evaluated across cost, schedule, performance,
and supportability. Use a 5x5 matrix; calibrate Likelihood and Consequence
criteria per program and document them in the Risk Management Plan (RMP).

## Lifecycle
1. **Identify** — workshops, gate reviews, ILA findings, EVMS variances,
   ISEA inputs.
2. **Analyze** — assign L × C and rationale.
3. **Plan** — mitigation, contingency, and trigger conditions.
4. **Implement** — assign owner, due dates, and budget.
5. **Monitor** — at every PMR / monthly review; close or escalate.

## Domain-Specific Risk Categories
- **ILS / Supportability** — provisioning gaps, TDP immaturity, training lag.
- **Acquisition** — requirements drift, contract structure, gate readiness.
- **Programmatic** — funding cuts, schedule slip, EVMS variance, manpower.
- **Technical** — design maturity, integration, cyber, obsolescence.
- **External** — industrial base, NSLC capacity, fleet schedule changes.

## S4ight Guidance
- When a user asks about risks, prompt for: program, milestone in scope,
  top L×C threshold to surface, and any known recent findings.
- For each risk, output: id, title, category, L, C, mitigation, owner,
  trigger condition, and the IMS/EVMS hook.
- Always pair a risk with the LCSP section it would update on closure.
