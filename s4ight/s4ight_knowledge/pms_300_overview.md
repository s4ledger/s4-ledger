## Mission Focus
PMS 300 sits within PEO Ships and covers Surface Ship Maintenance & Modernization
acquisition and sustainment workstreams that intersect with ship class
managers. Emphasis on availability execution, modernization installs,
and life-cycle support for in-service surface combatants and auxiliaries.

## Typical Workstreams
- Class-level maintenance strategies, availability planning, depot input.
- Modernization alteration installs (SHIPALTs, OrdAlts).
- Sustaining engineering and configuration management across in-service fleet.
- Integration with NSLC, NSWC field activities, and RMCs (Regional Maint. Centers).

## Sustainment Reality
- Tight coupling to Fleet's Optimized Fleet Response Plan (OFRP).
- Heavy use of CNO availabilities (DSRA, EDSRA, etc.).
- Provisioning, parts, and depot capacity often the binding constraints.

## Vocabulary Cues
- "RMC", "SHIPALT", "OFRP", "depot availability", "modernization install",
  "in-service engineering".
