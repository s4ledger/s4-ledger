## Mission Focus
PMS 385 supports surface ship support equipment, auxiliary systems, and
in-service engineering scope under PEO Ships. Day-to-day work intersects
heavily with ILS Supply Support, Sustaining Engineering, and Technical Data
elements.

## Typical Workstreams
- In-service engineering for auxiliary systems and support equipment.
- Configuration management and TDP currency across an installed base.
- Obsolescence / DMSMS resolution and engineering changes.
- Support equipment provisioning, calibration, and depot integration.

## Sustainment Reality
- Diverse legacy equipment baselines; CM discipline is critical.
- Rapid response engineering needed to keep fleet availability up.
- Strong ISEA partnership model.

## Vocabulary Cues
- "ISEA", "in-service engineering", "DMSMS", "obsolescence", "support
  equipment", "calibration", "auxiliary systems".
