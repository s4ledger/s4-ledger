# Acquisition Lifecycle (Adaptive Acquisition Framework)

DoDI 5000.02 establishes the Adaptive Acquisition Framework (AAF) with six
pathways. For PMS 300/325/385 sustainment-heavy work, the **Major Capability
Acquisition (MCA)** and **Middle Tier of Acquisition (MTA)** pathways are most
common, with sustainment governed by DoDI 5000.91.

## MCA Phases & Milestones
1. **Materiel Solution Analysis (MSA)** — pre-MS A. AoA conducted; ICD
   approved by JROC.
2. **Technology Maturation & Risk Reduction (TMRR)** — MS A → MS B. Refine
   requirements (draft CDD), reduce technology and integration risk.
3. **Engineering & Manufacturing Development (EMD)** — MS B → MS C. Mature
   design, complete TDP, integrate and verify the system. CDD approved.
4. **Production & Deployment (P&D)** — MS C → FRP/IOC. LRIP, OT&E, FRP
   decision, fielding.
5. **Operations & Support (O&S)** — IOC → disposal. LCSP execution,
   sustaining engineering, ILS in steady state.

## Key Documents
- **ICD / CDD / CPD** — JCIDS requirements documents.
- **Acquisition Strategy (AS)** — PM's plan for executing the program.
- **APB** — Acquisition Program Baseline (cost, schedule, performance
  thresholds and objectives).
- **TES / TEMP** — Test and Evaluation strategy/master plan.
- **LCSP** — see lcsp_overview.md.
- **PESHE / ESOH** — environmental/safety/occupational health.
- **ILS / IPS Plans** — element-by-element.

## Gate Reviews (typical Navy practice)
- **Gate 0–6** structure used by ASN(RD&A) for major programs.
- **Gate 4** (Pre-MS B), **Gate 5** (Pre-MS C), **Gate 6** (Pre-FRP) are
  highest-friction decisions.
- Each gate requires evidence of risk reduction, EVMS health, ILS maturity
  via ILA, and LCSP alignment.

## Common Failures
- Requirements (CDD) drifting from cost/schedule reality.
- TDP and ILS deliverables behind the design baseline.
- LCSP and IPS-element plans not synchronized at the milestone.
- Risk register not credible — too few risks, no quantified impacts.

## S4ight Guidance
- When a user mentions a gate / milestone, immediately frame the answer
  around required artifacts and ILA-relevant evidence.
- Tie acquisition decisions explicitly to downstream ILS impacts and the
  LCSP.
