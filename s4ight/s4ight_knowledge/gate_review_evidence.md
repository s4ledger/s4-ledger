# Gate Review Evidence Checklist (Navy ASN(RD&A) Gate Pattern)

Navy major programs typically run six gate reviews chaired by ASN(RD&A)
or the PEO. PMS 300/325/385 programs see these most often at Gates 4
(pre-MS B), 5 (pre-MS C), and 6 (pre-FRP).

This is the disciplined evidence pack S4ight expects to see assembled.

## Gate 4 — Pre-Milestone B

- Approved CDD (or current draft with JCIDS schedule).
- Refined Acquisition Strategy & cost estimate (DCARC / SCEA).
- Affordability analysis updated.
- TES, draft TEMP, draft SEP.
- Draft LCSP — IPS element coverage at "yellow or better".
- Initial ILA findings if conducted.
- Risk register with top 10 risks, mitigations, owners.

## Gate 5 — Pre-Milestone C

- Approved CDD; CPD in development.
- Mature LCSP — IPS coverage "green/yellow", no reds without a CAP.
- ILA conducted; CAP for any reds funded and scheduled.
- TDP maturity sufficient for provisioning; provisioning baseline frozen
  90 days out.
- Updated APB; affordability re-confirmed.
- EVMS health: cumulative CPI/SPI ≥ 0.95 trend.
- Cyber strategy and ATO path defined.

## Gate 6 — Pre-FRP / IOC

- Approved CPD.
- LCSP execution-ready; sustaining engineering funding through FYDP.
- ILA closed-out or in active closure; ISEA SOW(s) in place.
- DR / DMSMS process operational; metrics reported monthly.
- Training products fielded; manpower NEC plan executed.
- IMS shows steady-state sustainment cadence post-IOC.

## Anti-patterns

- "Pencil-whip" LCSP to all-green just before the gate.
- ILA reds present but no funded CAP.
- Provisioning still in flux at MS C → fielded gaps guaranteed.

## S4ight Output Skeleton

For any gate-review question, S4ight produces:
- A pre-flight checklist matched to the gate.
- A red/yellow/green call per artifact with the gap line.
- A recommended sequencing of the next 30 days of work to close gaps.
