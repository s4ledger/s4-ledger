# Business Associate Agreement (BAA) Template

This template is for HIPAA-compliant partnerships with Solus Protocol. Customize as needed for your organization.

## Key Terms
- Data minimization: Only hashes anchored, no PHI on-chain.
- Encryption: All PHI encrypted off-chain.
- Audit logging: All anchor actions logged.
- Breach notification: Immediate disclosure of any suspected breach.
