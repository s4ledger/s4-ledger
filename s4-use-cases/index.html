Core Verification Capabilities

Supply Chain Provenance & Counterfeit Prevention

Every part transaction — manufacture, QA inspection, shipment, receipt, installation — gets hashed and anchored. Creates an immutable chain of custody from OEM to fleet. Instantly trace suspect parts across every hand they've passed through.

Technical Data Package (TDP) Integrity

Every drawing revision, ECP, tech manual update, and engineering change gets anchored at the moment of approval. Anyone across any service verifies they hold the current, unaltered version. Creates an immutable revision timeline.

Maintenance Record Verification (3-M / PMCS)

When maintenance is performed and logged, the record gets hashed and anchored immediately. The XRPL timestamp proves exactly when it was created — it cannot be backdated. Eliminates gundecking and provides irrefutable evidence.
